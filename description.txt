v0.9.0 — Elemental Update (Public Testing)
Requires ARPG Enemy System.

The biggest addition in this update is the elemental affix layer. Weapons can now roll affixes that convert a portion of their damage to Fire, Cold, or Lightning, deal bonus elemental damage, or penetrate enemy armor and elemental resistances. Armor and accessories can roll physical and elemental resistance affixes that reduce incoming damage from matching sources.

All of this feeds directly into the elemental resistance system on enemies added by ARPG Enemy System. The two mods are designed to work as a pair — but ARPG Item System handles all its own UI, networking, and stat math independently.

Player resistances (Fire, Cold, Lightning, Physical) are displayed as shield icons next to the accessory slots, updating in real time as you equip and remove gear.

This is a public testing release (v0.9.0). Balance, affix values, and UI are still being refined. Reports and suggestions are appreciated. You can enable logging feature in client config (of ARPG Enemy System) to help testing feature.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ARPG Item System replaces Terraria's vanilla prefix/reforge system with something closer to what you'd find in an action RPG. Every weapon, armor piece, and accessory you pick up rolls random affixes — a prefix and a suffix with values tied to your current boss progression. The Goblin Tinkerer's reforge button is replaced with a proper reroll panel where you can lock affixes you like and reroll the rest.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Mod features:

- Weapons, armor, and accessories roll ARPG-style prefixes and suffixes on creation
- Affix magnitudes scale with boss progression — early-game rolls are weaker, late-game rolls are stronger
- Press C (default) while holding an item to reroll its modifiers for a coin cost
- Lock individual affixes to keep them across rerolls
- The vanilla reforge system is fully suppressed — no conflicting prefix rolls

All my mods are built with multiplayer and mod compatibility in mind
